## Project Overview
This repository contains a custom-built photography website developed for **Earl Raven** (also known as **Sasha DeBenedictis**).

The goal of this project is to create a refined, gallery-style website that presents photography in a calm, editorial, and timeless way. The website uses a monochrome interface (black, white, and gray only), allowing the photographs themselves to be the primary source of color and visual focus.

This project is privately developed and is not intended to be a reusable template or open-source project.

---

## Purpose
- Showcase photography in a professional, gallery-like format  
- Provide a custom alternative to hosted portfolio platforms  
- Maintain full creative and technical control  
- Serve as a long-term portfolio and client contact website  

---

## Page Structure

### Home
- First impression and visual introduction  
- Large hero image or minimal headline  
- Sets the tone and style of the website  
- Directs visitors toward the gallery  

---

### Master Gallery
- Curated collection of the strongest work  
- Primary portfolio page for clients  
- Mix of color and black & white photography  
- Clean, calm browsing experience  

---

### Archives
- Dedicated space for extended and categorized work  
- Shows creative depth, growth, and experimentation  
- Same visual style as the Master Gallery  

The Archives page may be organized into **subsections**, such as:

**By time or phase**
- Beginning Work  
- Experimental Work  
- Personal Projects  

**By subject or theme**
- Nature  
- Flowers  
- Animals  
- Cars  
- Food  
- Other focused subjects  

These subsections are flexible and may change over time as the body of work grows.

---

### Information
- Dedicated page about Earl Raven (Sasha DeBenedictis)  
- Artist statement and background  
- Style, mindset, and approach to photography  
- Notable projects, recognition, or achievements  
- Focused on identity and experience, not booking  

---

### Blog
- Client reviews and testimonials  
- Short write-ups about shoots or projects  
- Occasional updates or behind-the-scenes stories  
- Used to build credibility and trust  

---

### Contact
- Dedicated page for reaching out  
- Client inquiry and booking form  
- Collects name, email, phone number (optional), and message  
- Submissions send an email directly to Earl Raven  
- May optionally display public contact details  

---

## Style Direction
- Black, white, and gray interface only  
- Editorial typography  
- Minimal layout with generous spacing  
- No accent colors or visual clutter  
- Photography remains the primary focus
